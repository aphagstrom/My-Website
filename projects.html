Multi-scale modeling of Protein Dynamics

GitHub Repository

Created a coarse-grained 3D model of a protein from Cryo-EM data and refined it using Blender to remove artifacts. Ran FEM simulations to analyze large-scale dynamics, then mapped trajectories back to atomic resolution and performed MD simulations in OpenMM to capture finer motions. Generated synthetic Cryo-EM projections from the MD results, and used Bayesian inference to reconstruct a new 3D model with uncertainty estimates. Then repeat. This iterative pipeline combined structural refinement and dynamic modeling across scales.
